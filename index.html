Getting Started

Your 5-step AI productivity plan

Start here. You do not need to be technical — just intentional.

01

Audit where your time actually goes

Track one week of work in 30-minute blocks. You'll immediately see what can be handed to AI.

02

Pick one bottleneck to fix first

Don't try to automate everything at once. Solving one painful problem well beats tackling ten badly.

03

Choose the right AI tool for the job

Writing, coding, analysis, and voice all have specialised tools. Match the tool to the task.

04

Build a repeatable prompt or workflow

The magic of AI isn't one good output — it's a system that gives you good outputs reliably.

05

Measure, refine, and expand

Check your time savings monthly. Iterate on what works, drop what doesn't, and grow your AI stack.

Pro Tips

Habits that separate power users

Technical skill matters less than these daily practices when it comes to real productivity gains.

🎯

Be specific in your prompts

Vague instructions produce vague results. Give AI context, constraints, and examples to unlock its full potential.

🔁

Iterate, don't settle

Treat the first output as a rough draft. Ask AI to revise, improve, or approach it differently before accepting.

🧩

Break complex tasks into steps

AI performs better when given one focused subtask at a time instead of an entire project at once.

📋

Save your best prompts

Build a personal prompt library. A prompt that worked once is a repeatable system — not a lucky accident.

⚖️

Always verify outputs

AI can hallucinate facts. Use it to accelerate your thinking, but keep your critical judgement switched on.

🧘

Protect your deep work time

AI handles the shallow work so you can go deeper on what requires human creativity and judgement.
